EV800D VR Goggles

These goggles were a no brainer for the price. The goggles are one of the lowest priced goggles that have DVR capability. DVR enables you to record your flights and I wanted this feature so I could record my flight progress and see how I grow throughout my drone flying experinces! Another benefit to these goggles is that even though they are bulky they can be split into two pieces so you can have a friend view the live feed with you! For the price these goggles have a lot to offer.

FS-i6x: Dual Protocol Decision

The reason I chose this transmitter is because of the versatility the controller offers. It has the ability to switch protocols - which enables me to grow with the controller and it prevents lockdowns to specific brands. The protocols are AFHDS and AFHDS2 which are used by flysky controllers.

Eachine E010S

I choose this drone because it works with the transmitter I got and it came preassembled, which I wanted for my first drone. I took the same philosphy that you see with car enthusists and decided that having a working model to modify will be better start to the hobby rather than building a drone from the start(which could lead to multiple assembly problems possibly leading to a fruitless setup). This quad seemed to have enough punch and the frame gives the rotors durability (and saftey) as it has a guard. If I wanted to build a new quad I could salvage parts like the camera and frame too.
